Special characters (covered a little bit already) require a unique symbol to be properly represented - this always begins with the & character, followed by some letters and numbers. For example:

  • [extra space]    &nbsp;
  • [< and >]    &lt; and &gt;
  • [©]    &copy;
  • [left arrow]    &larr;

For a full list, see: http://www.webmonkey.com/2010/02/special_characters
